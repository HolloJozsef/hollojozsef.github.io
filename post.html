Ecommerce website

This project was started in order to improve my web development knowledge and enhance my Javascript skills on my own.

There are two types of users, admins and customers.

Admins can edit and add new products and categories while customers can purchase items, add reviews and ratings.

Further developments can include a second hand platform where customers are able to sell and edit their own products.
